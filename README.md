Artify – Online Art Marketplace

Artify is a web-based platform that connects artists and art collectors.
It allows artists to showcase, manage, and sell their artworks online, while collectors can discover, like, and purchase unique artworks from different artists.

The platform helps independent and local artists reach a wider audience beyond physical galleries.

🚀 Features
👩‍🎨 Artist Features

Artist registration and login

Artist dashboard

Upload artwork with title, description, price, and image

Manage uploaded artworks

Receive order notifications

Communicate with collectors

Track orders

🧑‍💼 Collector Features

User registration and login

Explore artworks from different artists

View artwork details

Like and save artworks

Add artworks to cart

Purchase artworks

Communicate with artists

Track orders

⚙️ System Workflow

When an artist uploads artwork:

Artist Uploads Artwork
        ↓
Image processed by Multer
        ↓
Image stored in Cloudinary
        ↓
Artwork details saved in MongoDB
        ↓
Artwork appears in Explore section
        ↓
Collector views and purchases artwork
